E-commerce Store

Website 2

The E-Commerce Store Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline the process of managing and operating an online store. The system is built to accommodate three key user roles: Store Administrators, Vendors, and Customers.
Store Administrators have full control over the platform, including managing users, products, orders, payments, and system settings. They oversee vendor activities and ensure smooth platform operations.
Vendors are responsible for managing their own storefronts, adding and updating products, processing orders, and handling customer interactions.
Customers can browse products, place orders, make payments, track their purchases, and leave reviews.
With a focus on user-friendly design and seamless functionality, the E-Commerce Store Management System aims to enhance online shopping experiences. It provides powerful tools for product management, secure payment integration, and order tracking, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience for all users.

Console-Based HMS

Application 1

The Hospital Management System is a computer program designed to streamline hospital operations as manual management becomes challenging. It's a time-saver for both patients and staff, eliminating the need for patients to wait in long queues. This system primarily deals with patient management, allowing the staff to handle various tasks like generating reports, suggesting medications, and calculating bills. The staff uses this system to perform basic patient data operations known as CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete). Additionally, they oversee the scheduling of doctors and appointments. Patients can access their prescriptions, medical reports, and bills through the system. Moreover, for common diseases, the system offers basic guidance. Its automation of patient information and medical records helps the hospital operate more efficiently, reducing errors and enhancing financial management.
